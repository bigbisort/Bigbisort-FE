Watermelon Cultivation in India

India exports watermelons to countries such as the United Arab Emirates, Nepal, Maldives, Bhutan, and Qatar. In 2023, India exported 56,869,700 kg of fresh melons and watermelons

Areas of Cultivation :

The top watermelon producing states in India are Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu. In 2021-22, Uttar Pradesh produced the most watermelons at 21.91%, followed by Andhra Pradesh at 19.49%, and Tamil Nadu at 9.77%

Harvesting Time

The watermelon harvesting season in India varies by region, but it's generally between July and August

  • North Indian plains:

    • Watermelons are planted in February and March, and harvested in July and August

  • Northeastern and western India:

    • Watermelons are sown from November to January

  • South and Central India:

    • Watermelons can be cultivated almost year-round

  • Punjab:

    • Watermelons are sown from mid-January to March during the Rabi season
